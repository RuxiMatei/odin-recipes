They taste like the best carrots you’ve ever had: but they only have 3 ingredients and take 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh herbs
  • Salt

How to do it

  1. Cover and cook for 4 minutes: no stirring!
  2. Uncover and cook for 3 to 4 minutes, stirring. Now you can actually saute! Saute comes from the French word “to jump,” so you keep the food moving as much as possible. Add the salt and herbs, and you’re done!
